"Rick and Morty" is an animated science fiction sitcom that premiered on Adult Swim in December 2013. Created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon, the show has gained widespread acclaim for its unique blend of dark humor, complex storytelling, and exploration of existential and philosophical themes.
